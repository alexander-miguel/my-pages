# tismo

A quiet corner of the web for notes, plans, and experiments. No name attached
— just the work.

## About this site

Most of what lands here starts life as a note in Obsidian or a standalone
page built for a single purpose. Rather than letting those sit on a hard
drive, this site makes them shareable. Expect it to grow slowly and
unevenly.

## Around here

- [Blog]({{ '/blog/' | relative_url }}) — occasional longer-form writing.
- [Training plans]({{ '/training-plans/' | relative_url }}) — structured
  plans for running and endurance training.
- [Adhoc]({{ '/adhoc/' | relative_url }}) — one-off pages, tools, and
  experiments that don't fit anywhere else.
